ABOUT US

LAB ANIMALS is a dance-punk band pushing the boundaries of electronic and punk music. Our sound combines aggressive punk energy with infectious dance rhythms, creating a unique sonic experience that gets crowds moving.

Formed in the underground scene, we've been crafting our distinctive sound through countless live performances and studio sessions. Our music is designed to make you dance while questioning everything.

Stay tuned for new releases, tour dates, and more from the LAB ANIMALS universe.
